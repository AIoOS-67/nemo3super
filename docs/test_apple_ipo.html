Apple Computer Inc. — IPO Brief

Key Facts

  • IPO Date: December 12, 1980
  • Exchange: NASDAQ (ticker: AAPL)
  • IPO Price: $22.00 per share
  • Shares Offered: 4.6 million
  • Capital Raised: ~$101 million
  • Valuation at IPO: ~$1.8 billion
  • Lead Underwriters: Morgan Stanley, Hambrecht & Quist
  • First Day Close: $29.00 (+31.8%)

Context

Apple's IPO was the largest since Ford's 1956 debut and created roughly 300 millionaires overnight — more than any company in history at that time. Steve Jobs, 25 years old, held about 15% of the company and became worth approximately $217 million the day of the offering.

Famous Quote

"The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do." — Steve Jobs
